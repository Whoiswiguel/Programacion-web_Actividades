Web Design Steps

  • Determine the Intended Audience
    The colors, images, fonts, and layout should be tailored to the preferences of your audience.
  • Determine the Goals of the Site
    Some common goals are sharing information, creating community, selling products, and providing services.
  • Determine the General Content
    List the major topics, sections, and pages needed for the website.
  • Determine the Site Structure
    Decide how pages will link together and how users will navigate the content.
